Lighting Designer

To the left you will find the overhead light plot for For Colored Girls Who Have Considered Suicide/When The Rainbow Is Enuf which was designed and drafted by Lighting Designer Frankie Harrell. My contributions to the drafting of the plot include dimensioning, detail views for TAB hang positions, and general cleanliness according to typical drafting standards.

Below you will find some paperwork that I created for this production, including Channel Hookup, Inventory Schedule, Color Count, and Gobo count, as well as a simple magic sheet I put together to help with the programming of the show.
