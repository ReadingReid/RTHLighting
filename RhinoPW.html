Lighting Designer

To the left you will find the overhead light plot for Rhinoceros which was designed and drafted by myself. It includes a total of 154 lighting fixtures, including Source4s, Strand 6" and 2k Fresnels, ETC Source4 Lustrs and Source4 Lustrs with Cyc adapters, VLX Washes, VL 1ks, VL 2.5k Spots, Chauvet Colorband Pix, units with Wybron Scrollers and units with Beam Benders, 24v LED Tape, and a Hazer.

Below is some other paperwork that I drafted including a Color Angle Concept, a Magic Sheet for focus, a Section drawing, a detail view for the LED Tape placement, a paperwork package including Channel Hookup, Instrument Schedule, Color Count, and Gobo Count, two magic sheets I made on the board for programming and for the show run, and some pre-production 3D renderings done in Augment3d.
