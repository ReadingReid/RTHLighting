Head Electrician

To the left you will find the overhead light plot for Disney's Descendants which was designed and drafted by Lighting Designer Nate Cain. As Head Electrician for this production it was my responsibility to lead my team of electricians to hang and cable all 150 conventional fixtures including ETC Source4s, Strand 6x16s, Altman PAR64s, and Altman 6" Fresnels, as well as work with my Moving Light Technician to make sure all 45 intelligent fixtures were hung/installed and cabled properly.

Below is some paperwork that I created for this production including Start-Up and Shut-Down sheets for the on-call electrician and board op during the run of the show. There is also the report template I would send out at the end of every Practicum class, which is when the majority of electrician work would be done, as well as the sign up sheet for our focus dates.
