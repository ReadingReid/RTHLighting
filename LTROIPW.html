Moving Light Technician

To the left you will find the overhead light plot for Let The Right One In which was designed and drafted by Lighting Designer BJ Wilkinson. As Moving Light Technician I was responsible for all 44 intelligent fixtures, including ETC Source4 Lustrs, VLX Washes, VL 1ks, VL 2ks, Chauvet Colorband Pix, Wybron Scrollers, and a Hazer. My job was to make sure each of these fixtures were hung and cabled properly, were given an available address, and patched properly into the board.
